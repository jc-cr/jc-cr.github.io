Trip summary

After my internship in Ohio I needed to ride my motorcycle back to Texas.
Since I was close to the east coast, I figured I might as well take the opportunity to visit some of the beaches.
So I set my target destination to Key West in Florida and started my trip.

I started my trip on 8/16 and reached Key West 8/25.
On 8/17-8/19, I camped in the Monongahela National Forest doing a 48 hour Vision Quest.

I don't have much else to say.
It started raining a lot after I left Myrtle Beach.
I almost got stuck in Charleston, SC after a downpour since the streets started flooding.
Juno beach was really nice.
The water there was blue and the sand was fine.
Apparently there was German U-Boat presence near there in WW2 times.
