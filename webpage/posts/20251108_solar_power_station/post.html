Solar Power Station

2025-11-08 Tags: project

Put together this solar power station using an old solar panel and Ebike DC batteries I had in the garage.

I rewired the batteries in parallel to get 12VDC, 36A.

The solar panel was setup on top of a tree house.

The batteries are stored in an old paper towel case I found outside.
I had to buy the charge controller and the pure sine wave inverter to get VAC output option.
