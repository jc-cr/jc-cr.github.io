Obsidian Notes to Webpage

2024-12-14 Tags: penning, legacy

Recently I setup the workflow for converting my Obsidian notes to HTML posts for my webpage.

Initially I wanted to do a single, centralized, document that would link to new posts I wanted to make.
I didn't think that would scale well so instead we (Claude 3.5 and I), setup a script that would generate posts individually on demand.

Check out the source code here: https://github.com/jc-cr/jc-cr.github.io
