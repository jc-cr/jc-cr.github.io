Trip Summary

The first part of my trip took me from Ohio to Key West, Florida.
See Ohio to Key West Travel Log.
Recently I completed the last part of my trip to get back to Texas.

Here is the breakdown:
- Key west -> Miami
- Miami -> Sarasota
- Sarasota -> Ocala
- Ocala -> Panama City
- Panama City -> Mobile
- Mobile -> Eunice
- Eunice -> Georgetown-ish

About 1596 miles total.
Averaging about 228 miles per day!

Didn't do much those days other than drive, eat, and spend my evenings working a research project I need to submit soon.

I did pick up Mike Duncan's 'Hero of Two Worlds' in Panama City.
I've been a big fan of his podcast's and have been meaning to read this book.
So far it's been a really great read.

I recently found out that the "first person to die by suicide on a live television broadcast" did it in Sarasota, Florida!
And she was from Ohio, the state I was just returning from.
What a coincidence!

The drive from Panama City to Mobile was really nice.
I crossed various bridges going over the large bodies of water.
